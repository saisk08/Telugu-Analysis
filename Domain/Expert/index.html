    About agreement percentages and majority

    Agreement percentage is defined as the percentage of participants that rated a pair a particular score (1 or 2 or 3 or 4 or 5). Therefore, agreement percentage is calculated for each score of each pair. If the maximum agreement percentage of a pair is more than 50%, then the pair has a majority.
